Compartmentalisation

We know what, where and when to write.

A general rule of thumb is 3-4 accounts that showcase your brand and highlights your business’ personality. It can be difficult to find the right balance between having enough accounts that will showcase your brand, but not so many that it becomes extremely time-consuming. As a result, we have three social media accounts, across Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.

Even our sponsors will share our success to inform their audience of us and the competition. This will result in mutual benefits and integrate with other forms of marketing, like website and social media pages.

The voice, tone, moderation and audience of each social media is tweaked to ensure that the communication is highly effective to all of our information. We call this strategy the “compartmentalisation of words through paronomasia.”

Pentafluid

The name Pentafluid is derived from two words- penta, meaning five, standing for the five uniquely talented members of the team who put in maximum effort to ensure victory, and fluid. The word fluid describes the dynamic nature of the team, and how we can adapt to the situation at hand to provide an appropriate response.

Pentafluid was built from scratch as a brand- our design language, termed Fluid Design, aptly mimics fluid behaviour across all elements of UI to provide a sense of brand identity recognisable across all platforms. The logo is a meticulous vector, with an isometric F hiding in the negative space to denote that simplicity is indeed the ultimate sophistication.
